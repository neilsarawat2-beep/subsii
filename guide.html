HOW TO PLAY

1. Select a game mode.

2. Choose your Beast and Trainer.

3. Battles are turn-based. Each turn, choose a move or item.

4. Moves have accuracy. If it fails, the turn is wasted.

5. Status effects like STUN, BLEED, SHIELD change battle flow.

6. Reduce enemy HP to 0 to win.

GAME MODES

1 PLAYER
Fight an AI opponent. Learn mechanics and strategies.
2 PLAYER
Local versus. Two players share keyboard and fight fairly.
TOWER MODE
Survival gauntlet. Enemies get stronger. No mistakes allowed.
ONLINE
Fight other players online. Same rules, real humans.
