SUBSI KI JANG // OFFICIAL SYSTEM MANUAL

HOW TO PLAY

Subsi Ki Jang is a **turn-based tactical duel system**. Each player controls one Beast and one Trainer. Victory is achieved by reducing the opponent’s CORE HP to zero.

  • Turns alternate between players
  • Each turn: choose a MOVE or ITEM
  • Status effects persist across turns
  • Strategy > raw damage

GAME MODES

1 PLAYER (VS CPU)

Battle against an adaptive AI. CPU uses smart logic: healing, stunning, buffing, and kill confirmation.

2 PLAYER (LOCAL)

Two humans. Same rules. No AI assistance. Pure skill matchup.

TOWER MODE

Endurance mode. Face consecutive enemies with increasing difficulty. Final fights are extreme.

ONLINE (EXPERIMENTAL)

Planned real-time multiplayer. Rule-locked, no randomness abuse.
